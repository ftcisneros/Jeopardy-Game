How to play Jeopardy!

Objective: Answer questions from various categories to earn points. The player will have their points added up and the highest goes on the leaderboard.

Game Setup

Categories: Game board has multiple categories, each with questions of increasing point values.

Gameplay

Starting the Game:

Player select a category and question value to begin.

Answering Questions:

Players will select from multiple choice answers formatted in the "Who/What" in Jeopardy fashion and then submit their answer

Scoring:

Correct Answer: Points for the question are added to the player's score.

Incorrect Answer: Points are deducted.

Ending the Game:

Once the player has answered all questions or want to finish the game, click the endgame button at the bottom

End of Game

Scores will be recorded and the highest 3 recorded scores and users will be displayed
